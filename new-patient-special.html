Credentials &
Qualifications

When Dr. Lee started our practice nearly 40 years ago, he made a commitment to learn from the best clinicians anywhere and to bring that knowledge back to our patients here in Fayetteville. In fact, it has been at the core of our success. Dr. Lauren Lee Polito saw this first-hand working in her dad's practice during high school, and she continued this commitment after graduating from dental school.

From the prestigious LD Pankey Institute in Florida to the Foundation for Advanced Continuing Education, Dr. Dave Lee and Dr. Lauren Lee Polito actively seek the best opportunities to learn. They have pursued dental knowledge across the US and Europe, and they are leaders in numerous dental organizations including the Georgia Dental Association and American Dental Association.

Affordable & Convenient
